01.

The Foundation: Mislata & High-Performance Swimming

Discipline is not taught; it is forged. For Jorge, this journey began in the chlorinated waters of Mislata. Competing at the national level, he learned the grueling necessity of consistency, the strategy of a perfect stroke, and the mental resilience required to win Spanish national championships. This athletic rigor became the blueprint for his professional life.

02.

Academic Excellence & Strategic Choices

Enrolling in Engineering at the Universitat Politècnica de València (UPV), Jorge treated his curriculum like a grand optimization problem.

By consistently ranking in the top 10% of his class, he earned the freedom to pursue the most challenging specializations, ultimately pivoting toward the intersections of structural integrity and computational modeling.

03.

International Mindset

Engineering is a global language. To master it, Jorge immersed himself in diverse cultures—from an Erasmus year in Warsaw to high-impact work placements in the USA.

With a C1 Advanced English certification and a high TOEFL score, he has spent his career leading cross-border teams and translating complex technical requirements into universal solutions.

04.

Specialization at Purdue & NASA Collaboration

rocket_launch
Aerospace Masters

At Purdue University, Jorge specialized in high-dimensional optimization. This expertise caught the attention of NASA, leading to a collaborative project focused on aerospace structural efficiency.

This was the turning point: the realization that the same optimization algorithms used to shave grams off a satellite could be used to optimize Neural Networks. Thus, his transition into MLOps and AI began.
